Sushin Shyam Fan Webpage

Sushin Shyam (born 13 February 1992) is an Indian music composer, instrumentalist, and singer primarily associated with Malayalam cinema.

He is also a founding member of the folk metal band The Down Troddence. His music contributions to notable films such as Varathan(2018) and Kumbalangi Nights(2019) have consolidated his standing in the industry. Notably, his work on Kumbalangi Nights earned him the Kerala State Film Award for Best Music Director in 2019.
